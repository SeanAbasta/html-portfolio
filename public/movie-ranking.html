The Best Movies According to Sean

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Top Gun: Maverick

If you liked the first movie, you're gonna love the sequel.
It's not mindblowing but it does instill nostalgia.
I give it a solid 8/10.

World of Kanako

A thriller/mystery Japanese movie about a detective investigating the disappearance of her daughter.
Contains lots of dark scenes and imagery so definitely not something for family night.
If you're into these things, I give it a big 9/10.

The Irishman

The best gangster movie I've watched in recent history.
We no longer get much of that genre nowadays especially with all time great actors and Martin Scorsese at the helm.
So if you're like me, grab a beer and enjoy this masterpiece on a good Sunday afternoon.
Easily a 9/10.
